About Stockie

Stockie is a powerful web-based platform designed for individual investors, traders, and financial analysts to access comprehensive stock market tools with ease. With an intuitive interface and modern AI-backed tools, Stockie empowers users to analyze, predict, and stay updated with real-time market trends.

Our Mission

To democratize stock market insights by providing affordable, user-friendly, and data-rich tools that anyone can use to make informed investment decisions.

Key Features

  • Data Analysis: Visualize and study trends, highs/lows, and stock movement with uploaded CSV data.
  • Predictions: Get basic AI-driven predictions based on historical price movement patterns.
  • Market News: Stay updated with curated news feeds from top financial news sources.
  • Chatbot: A simple assistant to help guide users through basic tasks on the platform.

Why Choose Stockie?

  • Modern, responsive, and beginner-friendly interface.
  • Personal login system for secure and customized experience.
  • Integration-ready design for potential future expansions (ML models, APIs, payment gateways, etc.).

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vanilla)
  • Backend: Python Flask
  • Database: SQLite
  • Libraries: Pandas, Feedparser, etc.
